About Me

I’m currently studying for an MSc in Data Science and AI for the Creative Industries at University of the Arts London. My work centres around using machine learning in visual art and generative creative practices.

Before this, I also worked as a graphic designer, photographer, content creator and translator.

My goal is to explore the more emotional and imaginative intersection between art and AI, and to promote the innovative application of machine learning in the creative industry.
